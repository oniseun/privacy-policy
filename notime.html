1. Interpretation and Definitions

1a. Interpretation

The words with initial capitalization have meanings defined under the following conditions. The following definitions shall have the same meaning regardless of whether they appear in singular or in plural.

1b. Definitions

  • User: The individual using the NoTime app.
  • App: NoTime - the application designed for efficient time management.

2. Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

2a. Types of Data Collected

NoTime collects the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Photo
  • Date of Birth

2b. Use of Your Personal Data

NoTime collects your full name, photo, and date of birth for personalization purposes within the app. This information is only stored locally on your device and is never shared with any third parties or external servers.

2c. Retention of Your Personal Data

Your full name, photo, and date of birth are retained within the app for as long as you continue to use the app. You have the ability to delete this information within the app settings at any time.

2d. Transfer of Your Personal Data

Your personal data is stored locally on your device, and no transfer to external servers or third parties takes place.

2e. Delete Your Personal Data

You have the right to delete your full name, photo, and date of birth data within the app settings at any time.

2f. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

NoTime does not disclose your personal data, including your full name, photo, and date of birth, to any third parties. Your information is kept private within the app.

2g. Security of Your Personal Data

NoTime takes commercially acceptable measures to protect your personal data; however, no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

3. Children's Privacy

NoTime is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you become aware that your child has provided personal information, please contact us so that we can take steps to remove that information.

NoTime may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external sites. Please review the Privacy Policy of any third-party site you visit.
